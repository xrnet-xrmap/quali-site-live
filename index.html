What makes quali.chat different?

  • Zero data-selling
  • Spam & troll resistant
  • Multi-chain
  • Ultra-secure encryption
  • Auto-join through your wallet
  • Total identity privacy
  • Security for your wallet

How it works

  1. 1

    Connect your wallet

    Use a read-only connection — sign a one-time nonce (not a transaction).

  2. 2

    Set your rules

    Choose tokens/amounts that grant access to each chat.

  3. 3

    Automatic access

    Verified token-holders join instantly; others don’t.

Designed for real-world use

  • Conferences & events
  • Investor groups
  • Creators & memberships
  • DAOs & governance
  • Token launches
  • IRL communities

Wallet safety

  • Read-only wallet connection
  • Sign a one-time nonce (not a transaction; costs nothing)
  • Sessions are device-bound and revocable

Identity

FAQ

Do you sell user data?

No. quali.chat doesn’t sell data.

Which chains are supported?

Ethereum and æternity are available. Solana and Polygon are coming soon.

Is the wallet connection safe?

Yes. It’s read-only and uses a signed one-time nonce (not a transaction).

Is there a web client?

Yes — download the app or use the web app where available.

Is it open source?

Parts of the project are open-source (e.g., mobile and website repos).
