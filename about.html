About

Quali.chat adds token-gated access to your chats automatically. It checks holdings, assigns roles, and keeps membership synced—no spreadsheets, no tickets.

Why tokens?

Tokens are portable, verifiable, and programmable. They are perfect for dynamic memberships and permissioning.

What you get

  • Automatic access & revocation
  • Simple drop-in integration
  • No sensitive data custody
